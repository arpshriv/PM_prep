## Round 1: Problem Space Understanding & Product Vision (HM Interview)

This is the FIRST interview. The hiring manager is evaluating:

### What They're Testing

1. **Do you understand the problem space?** — Can you articulate the pain points of internal data consumers (Sales, Marketing, Finance, Product teams) who need reliable business data?

2. **Do you have product vision?** — Can you think about a multi-year roadmap for data products? What does "democratizing access to Cloud Business Data" look like in 2-3 years?

3. **Do your examples demonstrate the right skills?** — Behavioral questions where your past work proves you can do THIS job.

4. **Can you think on your feet about product?** — Hypothetical product questions that test product instinct, even if not about this specific role.

### The 4 Prep Areas

| Area | What to Prep | Time to Spend |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **A. Role & Domain Understanding** | What is GCBP, UDL, what problems they solve, who the users are | 2-3 hours |
| **B. Behavioral Stories (STAR)** | 5-6 stories mapped to the JD's responsibilities | 3-4 hours |
| **C. Product Vision & Roadmap** | How you'd think about a multi-year data platform roadmap | 2-3 hours |
| **D. Hypothetical Product Questions** | Practice product sense questions for data/analytics products | 2-3 hours |
