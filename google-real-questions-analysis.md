## What the data tells you about Google's loop
- **"Favorite product + improve + metrics + strategy"** is the single most likely opener ‚Äî it's a 4-in-1 that tests Product Sense ‚Üí Analytical ‚Üí Strategy in one chain. **Have a polished, non-Google answer ready** (they often disallow Google products) that flows product‚Üíimprovement‚Üísuccess metrics‚Üíroadmap.
- **Design-for-a-vulnerable-segment** (elderly, kids, blind) is huge ‚Üí rehearse the **segmentation + accessibility-first** muscle (frameworks ¬ß1.3).
- **Estimation is heavily represented** ‚Üí Google still loves Fermi (frameworks ¬ß3.1). Drill 5‚Äì6 cold.
- **The exact JD execution scenario appears twice** ("customer wants off-roadmap feature, Sales went around you to Eng") ‚Üí prepare this verbatim (frameworks ¬ß4 + your cannibalization/trust stories).
- **"Self-driving in 5 years"** is a recurring strategy meme ‚Üí have one crisp futurist framework (where-to-play / second-order effects).
- **The Shorts view-time‚Üë / watch-time‚Üì RCA appears 3√ó** ‚Üí for your role this is a gift: it's literally the cannibalization problem you already own. Memorize a clean decomposition.

## Highest-ROI prep list (do these first)
1. **Favorite-product chain** (product‚Üímetrics‚Üístrategy) ‚Äî 1 polished non-Google answer + 1 Google answer.
2. **Estimation drills** ‚Äî 6 cold Fermis (storage, daily volume, TAM-of-a-named-product).
3. **YouTube cluster** (your role): price YT Premium ¬∑ Shorts view-time-up/watch-time-down RCA ¬∑ increase YT revenue/Music ¬∑ YT + genAI. Use your monetization/cannibalization stories.
4. **Design-for-segment** ‚Äî pick reusable structures for elderly / kids / accessibility.
5. **Execute-with-judgment** ‚Äî the off-roadmap-feature scenario + hold-the-launch scenario.
6. **Metrics/north-star** ‚Äî Goal vs Metric vs Operating-metric discipline (frameworks ¬ß3.2).
7. **Behavioral** ‚Äî map your STAR/GRAIL stories to: conflict-with-eng, influence-without-authority, missed-deadline, ethical, negative-feedback, motivate-under-pressure.

## YouTube/monetization cluster (memorize for your role)
- **Price YT Premium / How would you price YouTube** (asked 5+ times) ‚Üí cost structure ‚Üí value metric ‚Üí segment WTP ‚Üí cannibalization of ad-supported tier. Your WhatsApp MM pricing story is the analog.
- **Shorts view-time up, watch-time down** ‚Üí decompose: is total time up or just shifted? cannibalization of long-form (your incremental-rev-per-user framing); RCA = real vs measurement ‚Üí internal-vs-external ‚Üí segment ‚Üí funnel.
